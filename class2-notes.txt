
Environmental variables

VAR="hello" -> we capitalize by convention


use (()) to get bash to interpret information inside as a mathematical expression

use $(()) when you want to use something as a variable

Bash understands +-/%*

** exponentiation
<<,>> left shifting and right shifting
~ bitwise negation
&. ^, | are bitwise AND, XOR, and OR

Bash only works with integers. Usually only using addition and subtraction
